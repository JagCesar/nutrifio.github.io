Scan, Eat & Repeat.

Calories matters. For you.

Every day your body must be fueled with calories in order to function. Every day you burn calories even if you are laying in bed. People who excercise and workout burn more calories and must therefore have a greater intake of dietary calories, so the body can handle the loss.

However with todays easy to catch fast food, delis, sodas, snackbars and so on it's very common that you might feed your body with a hint of too much dietary calories per day, resulting in unhealthy weight gains.

We decided to give you a little help on the way of tracking your daily calories.

 

 

Super easy. Super fast.

Most apps needs a ton of skills to work, we hate it, you probably hate it too. Your daily routines of food and drink shouldn’t be wasted by lousy apps that requires a lot of know-how or attention to work. Nutrif.io does all the work for you.

Less features. Daily usage.

Nutrif.io is built for daily quick snapshots of your life. Wherever and whenever you are about to grab some food or a drink, Nutrif.io is there for you to help you keep track of your dietary calories. We spare you from additional features, this is about calories and nothing else.

Instant

The camera will fire up in no time when you start the app, with the small difference you don't even have to engage, just point towards a barcode and Nutrif.io do the rest.

Fast Lookups

With the help of our partners we give you instant and accurate access to the nutrition data behind the product barcodes.

*Typical lookup speed in good cellular conditions. (3G/4G)

One Touch

Only one action is needed, once a lookup have occured with success. Just hit the Add button in order to save it to the Health app.

Magical Slider

Have you just had a quick zip of your soda or simply half your snack? No worries we have a fantastic solution for that, just slide your finger across the screen (horizontally) and you will come to rest with your thoughts and needs.

Rest assured. More to come.

We are in hard work to bring even more fantastic features to your daily dietary calories counting. Next version will bring some really powerful yet simple to use features. But why not have a go already today and just lean back and let Apples automated update feature sort out the rest.

 

Apple Health Integration

Nutrif.io is made for health with full iOS 8 Health app integration. When something is scanned, you can choose to add and log the amount of dietary calories right to the Health App to easily and secure keep track of your health.

That means that your personal calorie intake will be yours and yours alone. No one has access to this data but you. Not us, not even Apple.

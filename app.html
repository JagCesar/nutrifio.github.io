Calories matters

Every day your body must be fueled with calories in order to function. Every day you burn calories even if you are laying in bed. People who excercise and workout burn more calories and must therefore have a greater intake of dietary calories, so the body can handle the loss. However with todays easy to catch fast food, delis, sodas, snackbars and so on it's very common that you might feed your body with a hint of too much dietary calories per day, resulting in unhealthy weight gains.
Therefore we decided to give you a little help on the way of tracking your daily calories intake. This is Nutrif.io

 

 

It's a one touch app.

Apps that requires a lot of handy work before usage sucks, we know that! Your daily routines of food & drink shouldn't be wasted by lousy apps that requires a lot of know-how or attention to work. Therefore Nutrif.io do all the work for you. Just point to your selected bar code and we do the rest. Then choose to add the current servings amount of calories to your Health app. Yes that's it!

Less features, Intense usage

Nutrif.io is built for daily quick snapshots of your life. Where and when you are about to grab some food or drink Nutrif.io are there for you to help you keep track of your dietary calories. Therefore we spare you from additional features, this is about calories and nothing else.

Instant Action

Just like Snapchat the camera is on when you start the app, with the small difference you don't even have to engage, just point towards a barcode and Nutrif.io do the rest.

Fast Lookups

With the help of our partners we give you instant and accurate access to the data behind the product barcodes.

 

One Touch

As mentioned earlier, there are only one action needed, once a lookup have occured with success just hit the Add button in order to save it to your Health app for logging.

Apple Health app Integration

Nutrif.io are made for health with full iOS 8 Health app integration. All dietary calories that shows up after a scan is anonomusly collected, if you select to hit the Add button the current displayed amount of calories will be securely stored within the Health app. That means that you're personal calories intake will be yours and yours truly. No one have access to this data but you, not us, not Apple.

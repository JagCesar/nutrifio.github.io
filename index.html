Meet nutrif.io

The fastest way to count your calories, yes really!

This is how it works: At any time you are about to grab a snack or a soda 1) start nutrif.io 2) scan barcode 3) add calories and Boom! you are all set. It's a one touch app! Nutrif.io keeps all your intake syncronized via Apple Health app (HealthKit) for privacy and convenience so you can change device at any time. Privacy is everything and your data is always anonymously handled and there are no setup or login required (or even possible). Nutrif.io is free and will make your calories counting a lot faster and easier without any hesitation.

  • Built for iOS8
  • Using HealthKit
  • Using CloudKit

It's ultra modern, it's fast, and it doesn't drain your battery.

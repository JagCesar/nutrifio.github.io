Apple Watch

The first app that really gets your Health App going. A remarkable fast and easy way to keep track of your daily calorie intake.

Scan barcodes. Truly as easy as it sounds.

We believe in design that makes an app work as charming as it should be. By adopting this conviction all over our design process we have made Nutrif.io as simple we could. Fire up the app and you are all set to scan, point your phone towards any nutrition related barcode and BOOM there you go - You are presented with the serving amount of calories, brand and a quick description. Need to adjust the amount? Just drag your finger horizontally and you are free to adjust in any direction. Once satisfied, hit the Add button to store the calorie intake to your Health App.

At any time, drag to search, for any kind of food.

Yes we are aware that not everything you eat have a bar code, therefore we added the industries most intuitive food search feature. Pull down, and you have the power to search any kind of food, restaurant or common food items.

Beautiful, predictive & smart.

We think we are on to something when we say that we are pretty predictive in our behaviours, especially when it comes to food. Why not combine that with the beautiful yet simple technology you already got in your pocket. We call it Predictions, and it's pretty awesome and makes your calorie counting even faster, better and smarter.

Link Bracelet

Crafted from the same 316L stainless steel alloy as the case, the Link Bracelet has more than 100 components. The machining process is so precise, it takes nearly nine hours to cut the links for a single band. In part that’s because they aren’t simply a uniform size, but subtly increase in width as they approach the case. Once assembled, the links are brushed by hand to ensure that the texture follows the contours of the design. The custom butterfly closure folds neatly within the bracelet. And several links feature a simple release button, so you can add and remove links without any special tools. Available in stainless steel and space black stainless steel.

Milanese Loop

A modern interpretation of a design developed in Milan at the end of the 19th century. Woven on specialized Italian machines, the smooth stainless steel mesh wraps fluidly around your wrist. And because it’s fully magnetic, the Milanese Loop is infinitely adjustable, ensuring a perfect fit.

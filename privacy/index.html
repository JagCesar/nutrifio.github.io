Privacy is important, also for us.


What is this nutrif.io app?
Nutrif.io is a unique, fast and super easy app that help you keep track of your dietary calories intake by giving you a one-touch user interface for adding calories to Apple HealthKit framework. Read more about HealthKit.

How private is my data?
Very. All data is collected using encrypted API's, then handed over to your locally storage on your own device and also synced with your iCloud account so you are able to change device at any time - if it's time to switch - lost - dropped or stolen. No one else but you have access to this data.

How do we use the information that we collect?
We don't. Since we don't collect anything.

How do we use the information that are collected upon a scan?
We only keep track of amount of scans, bar codes and location data for the scan in order for us to give you more accurate suggestions even without a scan in further versions of this app.

Sharing of your Personal Information
The are no personal information passing this app. It's your life and device, we like to keep it that way.

Cookies and Other Tracking Devices
We might use analytics with our website or apps, which helps us optimize the experience for you (basically, to make our website and apps better). This means that a cookie will be stored on your computer – this is a small text file that stores non-personal information, and this helps us know large scale statistics such as how often people visit our site, how they use it, etc. We dont know who the actual people are – just the trends of usage. Most browsers automatically accept cookies, but you can modify your browser settings to decline or delete cookies.

FAQs

  1. How can you fit all that technology into such a small size?

    I know, we often ask ourselves that same question! Our designers and engineers have done an amazing job sourcing all the smallest components and designed the internals of the card to offer an amazing experience packed into the tiny dimensions of a traditional credit card.

  2. Can I use my Plastc Card in an ATM?

    Yes. We have made sure your Plastc Card will work with all ATMs. We followed all the industry standard regulations when replicating the physical, flexible, and durable attributes of a traditional credit card.

  3. How can I contact someone at Plastc?

    You can contact us via email at [email protected] or call us at 1-650-618-9882. We look forward to hearing from you!

  4. How long does the battery last?

    There is an internal, ultra slim, non-replaceable battery within the device, (hard to believe, we know!), that will last approximately 30 days before it needs recharging.

  5. Can someone else use my Plastc Card?

    We’ve implemented security features to prevent unauthorized use of your Plastc Card. You can opt to enable a 4-digit PIN at the point of purchase. Additionally, your data is not accessible once your Plastc Card has been out of range of your phone for a specific period of time that you set.

  6. Is my Plastc Card flexible?

    Yes. We have worked very hard to ensure the Plastc Card maintains the same properties as a traditional credit card, so it will flex when it’s in your pocket and still work. Even the screen has flexible properties!

  7. My Plastc Card isn’t working. What should I do?

    Oh no! Please contact us at: [email protected] and we’ll help you out.

  8. Can you pair your Plastc Card with multiple devices?

    No. For security reasons we only allow your Plastc Card to connect to a single device.

  9. What colors is the Plastc Card available in?

    Plastc Cards are only available in black at this time.

  10. Can I see all my credit card details on my Plastc Card so I can use it when I’m entering those details for an online payment?

    Yes. We have made sure your Plastc Card is a true replacement so if you need your card details you can retrieve them from your Plastc Card’s e-ink touchscreen.

  11. How can I get my Plastc?

    Thanks for your interest! To pre-order a Plastc Card, click the pre-order button at the top of the page.

  12. Will my bank allow me to use a Plastc Card?

    Yes. Plastc works exactly like a traditional credit or debit card.

  13. Will I be charged immediately for my pre-ordered Plastc Card? Why?

    Yes. Creating and manufacturing products, especially those which have intelligent technology, gets expensive. Utilizing the revenue from your pre-order enables us to bring the best product possible to market in a timely fashion.

  15. Can I refer friends and family even if I haven’t pre-ordered Plastc?

    Yes! Visit to receive your unique share link. When a friend or family member clicks on the link and pre-order Plastc, you’ll also receive a $20 discount for your own Plastc.

  16. Will all my cards work with the Plastc Card?

    We have designed Plastc to work with all well-known card types: the traditional magnetic strip card, as well as the new Chip and PIN and contactless cards following a firmware update.

  17. Will my Plastc Card work with my Chip and PIN card?

    Yes. Plastc has the new Chip and PIN technology built in.

  18. Can I use my Plastc Card with contactless payments?

    Yes! We have made sure your Plastc Card will work with contactless payment technology. This will be activated with an over-the-air firmware update shortly after launch.

  19. How long will it take for Plastc to be delivered?
